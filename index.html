Welcome to Among Us

Crewmates must stay vigilant and vote the impostors out.

Impostors need to "kill" all crewmates while avoiding being caught.

You are an IMPOSTOR.

Try to kill ALL crewmates before they complete their tasks but avoid getting voted out. Make sure the kills are discrete so that crewmates don't discover your identity.

Different ways to kill crewmates (you can kill ONLY ONCE every 10 minutes):

  1. Take a selfie with a crewmate. It must be with ONLY ONE person per photo.
  2. Steal a crewmates personal item and put it in the tv room. (Text Alaa or Mo for them to confirm)
  3. Clink a glass with a crewmate. ONLY ONE person at a time. Text Alaa or Mo to confirm kill.

Note:

  • At the start of the game you do not know who the other impostor(s) is/are, but you may be able to figure it out if you see them performing kills. You can choose to approach and create strategies or keep yourself hidden.
  • If a meeting is called try to blend in with the crew and avoid getting voted out.
  • As an imposter, you can also call an emergency meeting to throw crewmates off.
